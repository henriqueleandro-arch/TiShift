Why This Matters

  • Qualify early: score fit before committing delivery resources.

Automation Coverage

Automated: schema conversion, load, sync scaffolding, validation.
AI-assisted: procedures, triggers, and CLR-to-app code stubs.
Manual: linked servers or deep non-portable logic.

Readiness Scoring Model

Use the first scan to separate fast wins from bad-fit opportunities before investing SA time.

SchemaTypes, collations, layout.
CodeProcedures, triggers, CLR, jobs.
QueryDialect and pattern gaps.
DataTemporal, FILESTREAM, edge types.
OpsCDC and cutover readiness.

Good Candidate Signals

  • Mostly standard schema with limited procedural logic.
  • Few or no CLR dependencies.
  • High automated plus AI-assisted conversion share.
  • Clear replication and cutover path.
